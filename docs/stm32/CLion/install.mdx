# 安装 CLion


#### 1️⃣ 从官网下载最新CLion安装包


<a href="https://www.jetbrains.com/clion/download/" class="source_download_button" target="_blank">前往官网</a>
<div style={{marginBottom: "20px"}}>
</div>

首先点击上方按钮，进入官方的CLion下载页面，点击Download下载最新版本的安装包
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/官网下载.png').default} alt="官网下载" width="600px" />
</div>

#### 2️⃣ 打开安装包进行安装
一、欢迎界面，直接点击下一步
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/安装1.png').default} alt="安装1" width="400px" />
</div>
二、选择安装路径，C盘够大的话还是建议使用默认路径
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/安装2.png').default} alt="安装2" width="400px" />
</div>
三、按照需求选择安装组件 （我全都要）
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/安装3.png').default} alt="安装3" width="400px" />
</div>
四、选择开始菜单文件夹，点安装即可
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/安装4.png').default} alt="安装4" width="400px" />
</div>
五、等待进度条完成
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/安装5.png').default} alt="安装5" width="400px" />
</div>
六、安装完成，会询问是否重启，选择否，然后点击完成
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/安装6.png').default} alt="安装6" width="400px" />
</div>


#### 3️⃣ 首次启动与激活

目前CLion已经完全非商用免费了，第一次打开CLion时小伙伴们按照如下步骤进行激活即可

一、欢迎界面，直接点击下一个

<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活1.png').default} alt="激活1" width="400px" />
</div>
二、勾选同意协议，点击继续
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活2.png').default} alt="激活2" width="400px" />
</div>
三、根据自己的意愿，选择是否与JetBrains共享数据，帮助提升软件体验，两个按钮点哪个都行
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活3.png').default} alt="激活3" width="400px" />
</div>
四、点击“免费非商业使用”
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活4.png').default} alt="激活4" width="400px" />
</div>  
五、点击“登录以进行非商业使用”
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活5.png').default} alt="激活5" width="400px" />
</div>
六、在弹出的网页中使用合适的方式登录，比如使用微信扫码登录
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活6.png').default} alt="激活6" width="400px" />
</div>  
七、第一次登录注册的账号会要求绑定邮箱，然后输入邮箱中收到的验证码，最后输入用户名，完成注册
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活7.png').default} alt="激活7" width="400px" />
</div>
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活8.png').default} alt="激活8" width="400px" />
</div>
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活9.png').default} alt="激活9" width="400px" />
</div>
八、若弹出联网许可，点击“允许”
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活10.png').default} alt="激活10" width="400px" />
</div>
九、回到CLion界面，点击“开始非商业使用”
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活11.png').default} alt="激活11" width="400px" />
</div>
十、至此，CLion激活完成，可以愉快地使用了
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center", alignItems: "center", marginBottom: "20px"}}>
<img src={require('./img/激活12.png').default} alt="激活12" width="400px" />
</div>



:::warning
商用行为请要求公司购买商用版，效率的提升对比购买费用来说是非常划算的
:::
